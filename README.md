# 📊 Budget, Sales & Analytics Dashboard

## 🗂️ Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard delivering a unified view of business performance across budget, sales, and profitability metrics. Designed for data-driven decision-making, the dashboard empowers stakeholders to explore, filter, and act on key business insights in real time.

---

## ✨ Features

- 💰 **Budget Analysis** — Visual breakdown of budget allocation across categories for clear resource planning and oversight.
- 📈 **Sales Performance** — Trend analysis and KPI tracking to monitor sales patterns over time.
- 📉 **Profitability Analysis** — Margin insights across product categories, subcategories, and regions to surface high and low performers.
- 👥 **Customer Analysis** — Customer count and average yearly income metrics to understand purchasing behavior.
- 🛒 **Product Analysis** — Identifies top-selling products and highlights items with the highest tax implications.
- 🗺️ **Geographic Analysis** — Map-based visualization of sales and profit by region to support market expansion decisions.

---

## ⚙️ Capabilities

- 🔍 **Interactive Filters** — Slice data by category, region, or time period for focused analysis.
- 🔎 **Drill-Down Support** — Navigate from summary-level data into granular details to uncover hidden patterns.
- 🎨 **Customizable Layout** — Adaptable visualizations and layout to fit different reporting needs.

---

## 🎯 Goals

- ✅ Deliver a concise overview of budget, sales, and financial performance.
- ✅ Surface meaningful trends and patterns from complex datasets.
- ✅ Enable faster, evidence-based decisions across the organization.
- ✅ Serve as a shared reference point for cross-functional alignment.

---

## 👤 Target Audience
Built for managers, analysts, executives, and decision-makers who need reliable, up-to-date visibility into business performance.
